**What was done**: Fixed video attachment in chat. Picked videos were loaded with
`item.loadTransferable(type: Data.self)`, which returns `nil` for movies (PhotosUI
vends a movie as a file, not raw data), so attaching a video silently did nothing.
Videos now load through a file-URL `Transferable` and read off disk; both composers
also show an error instead of failing silently.
